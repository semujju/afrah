About Us



Afrah Development and Relief Agency Uganda is a Voluntary Charitable Organization which was established in Uganda in 2005. Afrah is also affiliated to other NGO`s. The organization`s objectives include

Our Mission is to be a non profit making Ornization dedicated to empowering the Disadvantage people of the world through Emergency Relief and Participatory Development Programs based on human dignity, self reliance and social justice.

Our Vision is to work towards a general improvement in economical, social welfare, health and education of human beings to become a national value to which all section of community are geared

On going Programs

With help and support from our local and international partners, we have able to extend a helping hand to the needy through various activities with include the following:

Emergency Relief

These are programs aimed at assisting communities that are faced with natural calamities like floods, drought/famine and manmade civil wars.                              

Health and Medical Care

We are working with Jinja Islamic Hospital to extend medical services to patients. These services include: In-Patients and Out-Patients,Laboratory,Dental,Orthopaedics,sonography-Ultra Sound,Free Family Planning, HIV-Aids Counselling and Treatment, and Immunization.

Water and Sanitation

Clean and Safe water is provided to over homesteads, schools and Mosques across Uganda.

Education and Orphans Support

Under this program, we own and run two schools, these are Rabiha Junior and Rabiha High School. Our students are provided with education,feeding,accomodation,school uniforms and other scholastic materials.

Micro Aid Project

These are programs designed to assist needy communities to over-come excessive poverty through participatory ideas to foster development in order to improve their respective wellbeing.                                                                                            

Mosque Construction and Renovation

These are programs aimed at constructing new mosques and renovating old ones in various parts of the country.

Season Programs

These include Ramadan, Aqiqa and Qurban Programs. The beneficiaries of the Ramadan program are given food packs that contain Rice,Maize flour,Sugar,Beans and Cooking Oil. Under the Qurban program, cows are purchased and distributed to various Mosques,schools and Social Centres across Uganda for slaughtering,thousands are benefitted with meat.
